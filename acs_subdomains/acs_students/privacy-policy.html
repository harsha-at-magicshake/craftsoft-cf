Student Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At Abhi's Craftsoft, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect and use your data when you use the Student Portal.

1. Data Collection

We collect minimal information required for your training journey, including your Student ID, Email, and Phone number for authentication via OTP.

2. Use of Information

Your data is used solely to provide access to course materials, track your progress, and communicate important updates regarding your classes.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including Supabase's secure authentication and Row Level Security, to ensure your information remains private.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell or share your personal data with third-party marketing companies. We only use trusted service providers like Supabase for backend infrastructure.
