Course Curriculum

Learn user-centered design, usability, visual design, research, prototyping, and real-world product thinking - from fundamentals to advanced industry practices.

Beginner Level - UI/UX Foundations

Goal: Build correct mental models, terminology, and basic design skills.

1 UI & UX Basics

  • What is UI, UX, CX, and DX
  • Role of UI/UX in digital products
  • User-centered design principles
  • Design thinking fundamentals

2 Usability & Accessibility

  • Usability concepts (learnability, efficiency, errors)
  • Accessibility basics & inclusive design
  • Introduction to WCAG & accessibility mindset

3 Information & Structure

  • Information Architecture (IA) basics
  • Navigation patterns & site structure
  • Mental models & user expectations

4 Visual & Interface Fundamentals

  • Typography basics
  • Color theory & contrast
  • Layouts, grids, spacing
  • Basic UI components (buttons, forms, inputs)

Beginner Outcomes

  • Understand how users think and behave
  • Design simple, usable interfaces
  • Speak UI/UX terminology confidently

Intermediate Level - UX Process & UI Execution

Goal: Apply structured UX methods and create real product designs.

1 User Research & Analysis

  • Personas & user journeys
  • Task analysis & workflow analysis
  • Voice of the Customer (VoC)
  • Focus groups & user interviews

2 Ideation & Design Methods

  • Brainstorming & creative thinking techniques
  • Storyboards & design canvases
  • User stories & use cases
  • MoSCoW prioritization

3 Wireframing & Prototyping

  • Low-fidelity wireframes
  • High-fidelity UI design
  • Interactive prototypes
  • Mobile-first & responsive design

4 Design Systems & Consistency

  • Design systems & pattern libraries
  • Style guides & component reuse
  • Material Design & platform guidelines

Intermediate Outcomes

  • Design structured UX flows
  • Create wireframes & polished UI screens
  • Build consistency using design systems

Advanced Level - Product, Testing & Career Readiness

Goal: Think like a product designer and work at industry depth.

1 Advanced UX & Strategy

  • Usability heuristics (Jakob Nielsen)
  • Cognitive load & usability friction
  • Dark patterns & ethical design
  • Cross-cultural & global UX

2 Testing & Optimization

  • Usability testing methods
  • A/B testing & heatmaps
  • Accessibility testing
  • Iterative design & continuous improvement

3 Advanced Interaction & Systems

  • Advanced interaction patterns
  • Complex workflows & system thinking
  • Design management & collaboration
  • Working with developers & stakeholders

4 Emerging UX & AI

  • AI in UI/UX
  • AI content & image generation
  • Designing for automation & intelligence

5 Portfolio & Career

  • Case study creation
  • UX storytelling & presentations
  • Interview preparation
  • Freelancing & product team workflows

Advanced Outcomes

  • Solve complex UX problems
  • Design scalable, real-world products
  • Build a strong industry-ready portfolio
