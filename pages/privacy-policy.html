Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 31, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Abhi's Craft Soft collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you enroll in our courses, use our services, or contact us, we may collect:

  • Name - To identify you and personalize your experience
  • Email Address - For communication and course updates
  • Phone Number - For important notifications and support
  • Address - For certificate delivery (if applicable)
  • Payment Information - Processed securely through third-party payment providers

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and location (country/city level)
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver Services: Process enrollments, provide course access, and communicate updates
  • Improve Experience: Understand usage patterns and enhance our offerings
  • Communication: Send important notifications, respond to inquiries, and provide support
  • Marketing: With your consent, send promotional materials about new courses and offers
  • Legal Compliance: Fulfill legal obligations and protect our rights

3. Data Protection

3.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with restricted access
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Employee training on data protection

3.2 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide services and fulfill legal obligations. Course enrollment records are typically retained for 5 years after course completion.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors (Razorpay), email services, and cloud hosting providers
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: For typography (may collect anonymous usage data)
  • Supabase: For authentication and database (governed by Supabase's privacy policy)
  • Razorpay: For payment processing (governed by Razorpay's privacy policy)
  • Formspree: For contact form submissions

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

6. Cookies

6.1 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us improve your experience.

6.2 How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

6.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured format

To exercise these rights, contact us at team.craftsoft@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

If you access our services from outside India, your data may be transferred to and processed in India. By using our services, you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
