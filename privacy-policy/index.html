Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 21, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Abhi's Craft Soft collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, phone number, email address, and address for certificate delivery. We also collect course enrollment details, payment information, and communication records (inquiries, messages).

Additionally, we automatically collect technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and usage patterns when you visit our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to deliver services, process enrollments, manage payments, and generate receipts. It also helps us improve our offerings, communicate important updates, and fulfill legal obligations.

3. Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission and secure cloud storage with restricted access. Access is strictly limited to authorized personnel only.

4. Payment Information

Online payments are processed via secure third-party gateways. We do not store sensitive card or bank details on our servers to ensure maximum security for your financial data.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Data may be shared with trusted service providers (payment processors, email services) strictly for transaction processing or service delivery, or when required by law.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website uses services like Google Fonts, Supabase (for database/auth), and payment gateways. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies as they govern their own data practices.

7. Cookies

We use essential, analytics, and preference cookies to improve your experience. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some website functionality.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access your personal data, request corrections of incorrect information, or request deletion (subject to legal requirements). To exercise these rights, contact us at team.craftsoft@gmail.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our platform constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us at team.craftsoft@gmail.com or visit us at Plot No. 163, Vijayasree Colony, Vanasthalipuram, Hyderabad.
